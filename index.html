Fun Facts About Imo State

  • It was created on the 3rd of February 1976.
  • It is one of the states in the South East of Nigeria.
  • Imo is majorly inhabited by Igbo people.
  • Language spoken in the state is Igbo.
  • It is located in the heart of Igboland.
  • The capital of Imo state is Owerri.
  • It is one of the most densely populated areas in Nigeria.
  • Its famous nickname: The eastern heartland of Nigeria.
